Welcome

Welcome to shopping lists, your one stop to make your shopping list. Whether at home, a break at work, your friend's house, or even at the grocery store itself, we will be your companion to create, edit, and check off all your shopping list items. Ending the days of forgetting your paper list at home or being away from the house, thinking of something you may need, just to forget before you get home.

Our Mission

Our goal here at Shopping lists is to help you improve your shopping experience, a task so central to life as we know it. We also hope to help everyone cut down that "carbon footprint" just a little by cutting out paper lists from your shopping experience. With our easy to use layout we hope you never have to touch that paper and pencil at the supermarket ever again!
